Kind Words

“Sometimes in your career, you find yourself in a place, or a role, or an organization in which, for whatever reason, you are not thriving. Admitting that you are struggling is difficult and it can raise existential questions: Am I good enough? What is my purpose? Did I make a bad choice? Monisha was a friend and adviser to me when I was in this vulnerable state and I was able to dig deep into these issues without judgment or fear and ultimately, transition to a role where I am happy and dare I say, thriving. And, Monisha also helped me recognize that these difficult moments in a career journey are not only normal, but essential, to grow into the leader you are meant to be.”
